Someone wants to change their flight. What should I do?
Reach out to Val. She can check what their flight currently costs, if there's money left in their travel budget, if they can change their flight at all, and if it will be at the responsibility of the new hire financially.
What if there is a room change for sessions?
Send out comms in the Slack channel with the new room. Don't worry about updating the website — just keep the Slack updated.
Do GTM new hires need their laptops for Days 2 and 3?
Not 100% necessary, but a nice to have.
Does 201 join us for dinner and activity?
Yes, group dinner/activities are inclusive of all Grafanistas in attendance.
